What I Do

I am currently pursuing a postgraduate degree in Applied Mathematics and my research is in the star-studded field of astronomy (not to be confused with astrology). What's the difference between the two you ask? Uhm, let's see.. Well, one studies the universe beyond earth's atmosphere and the other one is what people use to justify their undesirable personality traits (I'm a proud Leo by the way).
My research work focuses mainly on investigating the environmental factors that affect star formation in and around galaxy clusters at low redshifts (z ~ 0.15 - 0.3) using data observations obtained from the MeerKAT Radio Telescope.
